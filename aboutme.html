About Me

A Sydney that, in fact, does not live in Sydney.

Sydney's the name and creating's the game.

I'm a cis-female (she/her/hers) computer science major who's currently obsessed with broadway musicals. I'm a big supporter of the LGBTQIA+ community and an advocate for mental health support. Love is love. Our minds are just as important as our bodies.

Proud to be a member of the Hufflepuff house, and I alternate between being the ISTP, ISFP, ISTJ, and ISFJ personality types based on the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator. If elemental bending was a real thing, I'd totally be an airbender.

I love hazelnut-flavored things, and I enjoy playing guitar, rollerskating, tennis, fruit-picking, and biking.

Special thanks to those who got me here today.

Including but not limited to (and in no particular order):
  • My mom, dad, sister, and brother-in-law, who always had my back no matter what I chose to do.
  • Fan, who's always been incredibly accepting and willing to drive to me from miles away in the middle of the night.
  • Kirollos, who's been a constant friend in the moments where life got me down.
  • Qasim, who's legitimately one of the nicest people on the planet and became a great friend when I entered university.
  • Spencer, my camp director from years ago, who reminded me to be patient with myself when comparing myself to others.
